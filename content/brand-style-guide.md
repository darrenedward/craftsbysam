# Crafts By Sam — Brand Style Guide

**Last Updated:** 2026-05-31
**Status:** Official Brand Identity (v1.0)
**Owner:** CMO

## Brand Identity

**Crafts By Sam** is a New Zealand-based handmade crafts business specializing in personalized glassware, epoxy resin creations, and custom gifts. Every piece is handcrafted by Sam from her home studio with love, care, and attention to detail.

**Brand Essence:** Warm, personal, proudly handmade, authentically NZ-made

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Brand Colors

**Deep Pine Green** `#2D5A27`
- Use: Primary brand color, headings, key CTAs, logo accents
- Hex: #2D5A27
- RGB: 45, 90, 39
- Conveys: Natural, grounded, NZ bush, craftsmanship

**Warm Earth Brown** `#8B5A2B`
- Use: Secondary accents, borders, decorative elements
- Hex: #8B5A2B
- RGB: 139, 90, 43
- Conveys: Warmth, natural materials, handmade touch

**Natural Cream** `#F5F1E6`
- Use: Backgrounds, negative space, soft highlights
- Hex: #F5F1E6
- RGB: 245, 241, 230
- Conveys: Warmth, craft paper, natural canvas

### Supporting Colors

**Charcoal** `#3D3D3D`
- Use: Body text, primary content
- Hex: #3D3D3D
- RGB: 61, 61, 61

**Soft Grey** `#E8E8E8`
- Use: Light borders, subtle dividers
- Hex: #E8E8E8
- RGB: 232, 232, 232

**Accent Gold** `#D4A853`
- Use: Special accents, premium feel, wedding collection highlights
- Hex: #D4A853
- RGB: 212, 168, 83

### Color Usage Guidelines

**DO:**
- Use Deep Pine Green as the primary brand color
- Pair Earth Brown with Natural Cream for warmth
- Reserve Gold for premium/wedding collections only
- Ensure text contrast meets WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 minimum)

**DON'T:**
- Use the deprecated pink/plum palette from previous drafts
- Mix multiple bright colors — stay with the earthy NZ aesthetic
- Use Pure Black (#000000) — always use Charcoal for softer warmth

## Typography

### Primary Font: **Lora** (Serif)

**Usage:** Headlines, logo text, elegant touches
- Available via: Google Fonts
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 600 (SemiBold), 700 (Bold)
- Style: Classic, readable, handcrafted feel

**Use for:**
- Logo text "Crafts By Sam"
- Headlines (H1, H2, H3)
- Pull quotes
- Elegant product names

### Secondary Font: **Open Sans** (Sans-Serif)

**Usage:** Body text, UI elements, information-heavy content
- Available via: Google Fonts
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 600 (SemiBold)
- Style: Clean, modern, highly readable

**Use for:**
- Product descriptions
- Body copy
- Navigation
- Forms and UI text

### Font Hierarchy

**H1 (Page Title):** Lora Bold, 36px, Deep Pine Green
**H2 (Section Header):** Lora SemiBold, 28px, Deep Pine Green
**H3 (Subsection):** Lora SemiBold, 22px, Earth Brown
**Body Text:** Open Sans Regular, 16px, Charcoal
**Small Text:** Open Sans Regular, 14px, Charcoal
**CTA Buttons:** Lora SemiBold, 16px, Natural Cream background, Deep Pine Green text

## Logo Usage

### Logo Variations

**1. logo-horizontal.svg**
- Use: Website headers, business cards, letterheads
- Minimum width: 200px
- Clear space: 50% of logo height on all sides

**2. logo-icon.svg**
- Use: Social media avatars, favicons, app icons
- Size: Square format
- Minimum size: 64px × 64px

**3. logo-stacked.svg**
- Use: Product packaging, vertical layouts, stamps
- Minimum width: 150px
- Clear space: 50% of logo height on all sides

### Logo Color Rules

**Preferred:** Full color (Deep Pine Green + Earth Brown)
**Alternative:** Single color (Deep Pine Green only)
**Reverse:** White or Natural Cream on dark backgrounds

**DO NOT:**
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Change the font or spacing
- Place on busy/clashing backgrounds
- Use low-resolution versions

## Brand Voice

### Tone and Personality

**Always:**
- Warm and personal ("I'm Sam," not "We are Crafts By Sam")
- Proud of craft and handmade quality
- Celebrate NZ-made and small business story
- Use emoji naturally but not excessively (2-4 per caption)

**Never:**
- Corporate or overly formal language
- Generic templated responses
- Making promises about products you can't deliver
- Spammy hashtag behavior

### Voice Examples

**✅ GOOD:** "Hey there! I'm Sam, and I poured my heart into this custom wine glass just for you. Each piece is handmade right here in NZ with love and care."

**❌ BAD:** "Crafts By Sam is a leading provider of personalized glassware solutions. Our quality assurance process ensures..."

### CTA Guidelines

- Clear, specific calls to action in every piece of content
- Rotate CTAs: "Link in bio," "DM to order," "Comment below," "Shop now"
- Make it easy to take the next step
- Avoid passive language

## Photography and Imagery

### Style Guidelines

**Product Photography:**
- Natural lighting, soft shadows
- Natural Cream or light wood backgrounds
- Show scale with props (wine glasses, hands, natural elements)
- Highlight handmade craftsmanship details

**Lifestyle Photography:**
- Warm, cozy home settings
- Natural NZ scenery when relevant
- Diverse representation of customers using products
- Authentic moments, not overly staged

**Color Treatment:**
- Warm tones, slightly desaturated
- Avoid harsh filters
- Maintain consistency across all platforms

## Social Media Visual Standards

### Instagram

**Post Templates:**
- Aspect Ratio: 1:1 (1080×1080px) or 4:5 (1080×1350px)
- Background: Natural Cream #F5F1E6 or subtle gradient
- Accent: Deep Pine Green borders or Earth Brown decorative elements
- Logo: Use icon version in bottom-right corner
- Typography: Lora for headlines, Open Sans for body text

**Story Templates:**
- Aspect Ratio: 9:16 (1080×1920px)
- Background: Natural Cream with Earth Brown accents
- Logo: Place at top or bottom, ensure visibility
- Interactive elements: polls, quizzes, swipe-ups use brand colors

**Profile Avatar:**
- logo-icon.svg (195×195px minimum)
- Single color (Deep Pine Green) for clarity

**Cover Banner:**
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 (banner dimensions vary by platform)
- Background: Natural Cream with Deep Pine Green logo
- Tagline: "Handmade Personalized Gifts from NZ"

### Facebook

**Post Images:**
- Aspect Ratio: 1:1 or 4:5 (same as Instagram)
- Consistent color palette
- Include website URL or CTA

**Cover Photo:**
- 820×312px (desktop), 640×360px (mobile)
- Logo centered or left-aligned
- Contact information visible

**Listing Images:**
- Main image: 800×600px minimum

- Product centered, well-lit

**Template Structure:**
- Headline: Lora Bold, Deep Pine Green

- HTML template with brand colors
- Clear sections with Earth Brown dividers

**Listing Images:**
- 1:1 or 4:5 aspect ratio

## DO's and DON'T's

### ✅ DO

- Use the official color palette (Deep Pine Green, Earth Brown, Natural Cream)
- Use Lora for headlines and Open Sans for body text
- Keep the brand voice warm, personal, and proud of handmade
- Include the NZ-made story where relevant
- Show the human face behind the business (Sam as the maker)
- Use emoji naturally and sparingly (2-4 per post)
- Ensure all text meets accessibility contrast standards

### ❌ DON'T

- Use the deprecated pink/plum palette
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Use Pure Black or overly harsh colors
- Be corporate or overly formal
- Overuse emoji or hashtags
- Make promises about products you can't deliver
- Use generic stock photos without context
- Ignore accessibility guidelines

## Brand Applications Checklist

Before creating any branded asset, verify:

- [ ] Colors are from the official palette
- [ ] Typography follows the hierarchy (Lora + Open Sans)
- [ ] Logo usage complies with guidelines
- [ ] Brand voice is warm and personal
- [ ] Accessibility standards met (contrast 4.5:1 minimum)
- [ ] NZ-made story included where relevant
- [ ] CTA is clear and specific
- [ ] Asset quality is high-resolution (300 DPI for print, 72 DPI for web)
